Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 27, 2026

Codeman does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or usage information. Your code and your privacy are yours alone.

Overview

Codeman is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app operates entirely on your device. We have no servers that receive your data, no analytics tracking your behavior, and no interest in your personal information.

Data We Collect

None. Codeman does not collect any data whatsoever. Specifically:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No usage data or analytics
  • No crash reports or diagnostics
  • No device identifiers or fingerprinting
  • No location data
  • No browsing or search history
  • No cookies or tracking technologies

Your Code

Any code you write, edit, or view in Codeman stays on your device or on the remote servers you choose to connect to. We never access, read, copy, or transmit your code. Your intellectual property remains entirely yours.

Network Connections

Codeman may connect to remote servers (via SSH or HTTPS) only when you explicitly configure a connection. These connections are made directly between your device and your server. We do not proxy, intercept, or monitor these connections in any way.

Third-Party Services

Codeman does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app contains no ads and no third-party SDKs that collect data.

Support Contact Form

If you choose to contact us through the support form on this website, the information you voluntarily provide (name, email, message) is processed by Formspree and delivered to us via email. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any data, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy. Codeman is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update this page with the new effective date. Given our commitment to collecting no data, we do not anticipate significant changes.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via our support contact form.
